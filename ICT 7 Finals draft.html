About URSM LABORATORY SCHOOL

URSM LABORATORY SCHOOL is a center of learning excellence committed to nurturing students to become globally competitive individuals. The school offers a variety of academic programs, laboratory-based activities, and student-centered learning experiences.

We aim to promote intellectual growth, moral integrity, and leadership among our learners.

Where Are We Located?

URSM LABORATORY SCHOOL is located within the University of Rizal System campus, offering a safe and accessible environment for students.

Address: University of Rizal System, Morong, Rizal, Philippines

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What programs does the school offer?

A: We offer a complete K-12 curriculum with an emphasis on laboratory-based science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) activities, preparing students for university studies.

Q: When is the application period for the next school year?

A: The application period typically runs from February to April. Please check the official URS website for exact dates and formal announcements regarding entrance examinations.

Q: Is transportation provided?

A: The school does not provide dedicated transportation services. However, the campus is easily accessible via public utility vehicles and is centrally located within Morong, Rizal.
